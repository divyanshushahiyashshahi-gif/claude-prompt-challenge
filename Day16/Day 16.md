# Day 16 – Stock Fundamental Research Skill

## Skill Name
stock-fundamental-research

## Description
A Claude custom skill that analyzes Indian and global listed companies using fundamental analysis, financial statements, valuation metrics, business quality, competitive advantages, risks, and growth prospects.

## Objective
To build a reusable AI skill capable of generating professional stock research reports while avoiding investment advice.

## Features

- Quick Take Analysis
- Deep Dive Analysis
- Peer Comparison
- Pros & Cons Review
- Portfolio Fit Analysis
- Financial Health Evaluation
- Ownership Analysis
- Valuation Assessment
- Dashboard-style Reporting

## Key Metrics Covered

- CMP
- Market Cap
- P/E Ratio
- P/B Ratio
- EV/EBITDA
- Revenue CAGR
- Profit CAGR
- EPS CAGR
- EBITDA Margin
- Net Profit Margin
- ROE
- ROCE
- Debt to Equity
- Interest Coverage
- Current Ratio
- Free Cash Flow
- Promoter Holding
- FII/DII Trends

## What I Learned

- Stock fundamental analysis workflow
- Financial ratio interpretation
- Company valuation techniques
- Peer comparison methods
- Building reusable Claude Skills
- Creating structured AI research systems
