# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Objective

Generate a Customer & MVP Blueprint using Claude based on the Startup Validation Report.

## Startup

**PrepPilot AI** – An AI-powered interview preparation platform for college students and fresh graduates.

## Tasks Completed

* Set Claude effort level to Low.
* Continued in the previous conversation.
* Submitted the Startup Validation Report.
* Generated the Customer & MVP Blueprint.
* Reviewed:

  * Executive Summary
  * Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
  * Buyer Persona
  * Top 10 Customer Pain Points
  * Customer Journey
  * Customer Objections
  * Buying Triggers
  * MVP Recommendation
  * MoSCoW Prioritization
  * Pricing Hypothesis
  * Top 5 Risks
  * 30-Day MVP Plan
  * Founder Action Sheet
  * Final Scores & Verdict

## Key Learnings

* Clearly identifying the target customer improves product decisions.
* Building only essential MVP features reduces development time.
* Customer pain points should drive feature prioritization.
* MoSCoW prioritization helps focus on high-impact features.
* Validation before development minimizes product risk.

## Deliverables

* Customer & MVP Blueprint Report
* Screenshots of important sections
* Startup Validation Report

## Reflection

This activity improved my understanding of customer research, MVP planning, product prioritization, pricing strategy, and startup validation.
