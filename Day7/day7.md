# Day 7 - Claude Usage Strategy

## Objective
Learned how to choose the right Claude model and effort level for different tasks.

## Recommended Primary Model
Claude Sonnet

## Recommended Effort Level
Standard

## Key Learnings

- Haiku is best for quick lookups and simple tasks.
- Sonnet is best for daily coding, learning, and research.
- Opus is best for complex reasoning and high-stakes work.
- Standard effort handles most daily tasks effectively.
- High and Max effort should only be used when deeper reasoning is required.
