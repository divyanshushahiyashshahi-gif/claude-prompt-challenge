# Day 24/60 — AI Startup Business Consultant

## Objective
Used AI as a Co-Founder, YC Advisor, Growth Strategist, and Business Consultant to evaluate a startup's long-term business potential.

## What I Built
- Startup Summary
- Customer & MVP Analysis
- Value Proposition
- Business Reality Check
- Business Model Canvas
- Revenue & Pricing Strategy
- Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy
- Customer Acquisition Strategy
- First 100 Users Plan
- Competitive Position & Moat
- Reverse SWOT Analysis
- Investment Scorecard (0–100)
- Founder Action Sheet
- Visual Business Dashboard
- Sustainability Verdict

## Key Learnings
- A great product without distribution rarely succeeds.
- Revenue and customer retention matter more than vanity metrics.
- Strong execution and continuous validation reduce business risk.
- Pricing, GTM, and customer acquisition are as important as product development.
- Sustainable startups solve real problems with scalable business models.

## Outcome
Generated a complete investor-style business strategy report, including revenue model, GTM plan, competitive analysis, investment scorecard, visual dashboard, and actionable founder recommendations.
